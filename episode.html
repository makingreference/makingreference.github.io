Jumping Joe Beyrle

A 60-second look at the paratrooper who fought behind enemy lines, survived capture, escaped, and kept fighting.

Format: Vertical short · Source: History Minute / Making Reference

About this episode

Dropping into Normandy on D-Day was only the start. This episode condenses Joe Beyrle’s wild war— from sabotage missions and capture, to escaping a POW camp and fighting alongside the Red Army.
