About

What is Making Reference?

Making Reference History Minute is about the men and women who risked everything to put stop to the evil and madness of the the powers that wanted to end freedom. This short-form history project built around one idea: if you give someone sixty seconds, you can give them a story they never forget.

Each History Minute episode focuses on a single moment, mission, or person from the Second World War. The visuals are styled to try and portray the action and a focus on the life and death human stakes.

The series covers hundreds of stories across the European and Pacific theaters, from famous operations to the forgotten moments of the war that deserve to be remembered.

  • 60–90 second episodes
  • Scripted, narrated, and editedt to bring you elements you may have never heard before.
  • Optimized for TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, and Facebook
