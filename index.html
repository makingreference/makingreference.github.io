WWII Stories Told in 60 Seconds

Making Reference’s History Minute is a short-form WWII storytelling project. The full website experience is still in development, but the archive and episode pages are live.

Episodes will be released weekly featuring stories about World War II you may have never heard. From the big battles to the small facts that may have changed the outcome of the largest conflict the world has ever seen.

Thanks for checking out the project — more is coming soon.
